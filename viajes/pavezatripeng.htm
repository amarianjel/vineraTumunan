Mount Paveza trip

 

 

Day 1. Arrive at lodge, welcome drink followed by 3 course dinner with fine wine.

 

Day 2. Breakfast and then mount the horses. The ride will take you through native forest where you will see several different types of rare species of fauna, and possibly a fox, wild boar, and flocks of Tricahues (brightly coloured squawking parrots). You leave the horses just above the tree line and make your way to the summit on foot. At the top of the mountain (Mt Poveza (2050m)) the view is stunning and in the distance you can see the Universidad ice field and 2 volcanoes.

That evening you will camp by a well apointed private mountain refugio, with hot showers and modern bathroom facilities.

 

Day 3. Breakfast around the campfire, then hike back to the lodge via a beautiful waterfall.


Note. similar journeys over shorter periods are available.

 

Maximum 6 people (3 days, 2 nights). Includes 1 night at the lodge, a full days riding and half a days hiking, and a night camping in an exclusive mountain refugio. meals and wine.

Price USD$310 ($155,000 pesos Ch. per person)

  • Beach towels
  • Sturdy walking shoes
  • Jeans
  • Long sleeve shirts
  • Lightweight wind jacket
  • Torch
  • Sun cream
  • Hat
  • Sunglasses
  • Swimming trunks
