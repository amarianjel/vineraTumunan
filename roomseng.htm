Tumuñan Lodge Programs

The lodge has 4 bedrooms, all with private bathroom. The beds have high quality mattresses and linen with feather pillows and duvets. The lodge serves lunch and dinner. Every day we offer a different menu, and use fresh ingredients as much as possible. We are happy to accommodate people with special dietary requirements, but please let us know in advance to your stay of these requirements. The lodge also has a carefully selected wine list and a bar.

 

The below programs are examples of itineraries we offer at the Lodge. We are happy to create tailor made programs for individuals and groups Please contact us in advance if you would like to make your own program or learn more about the activities and excursions we offer. Individual and groups. Prices are based on twin occupancy

 

1. Weekend programme (3 days, 2 nights) USD$280 ($140.000 Ch. pesos) per person. Program includes a half-day's horse riding with barbecue, and all meals and non-alcoholic drinks. Transfers NOT INCLUDED.
 
2. Mount Paveza program (3 days, 2 nights) from US$300 ($165,000 pesos Ch.) . Includes 1 night at the lodge, a full days riding and hiking, and a night camping in the mountains.
 
3. Adventure program (6 days, 5 nights) US$1188 ($664,000 Ch. pesos) per person, overnight horse riding trip in the Andes, trekking in the valley of the dinosaurs and climbing Cerro Alto Del Padre (3400m). All meals, drinks and wine included.

 

4. Big canyon program. (5 days, 4 nights) USD$730 ($365,000 Ch. pesos per person). If you enjoy the experience of being in the wilderness far from civilization, riding along rocky ridge lines in the high Andes, camping under the stars by a fire in simple shepherd shelters, and after the expedition, return to the lodge and enjoy the hot tub, comfy rooms and soft beds, showers and delicious food, then the "big cvalley program" may be what you are looking for.

 

 

*All programs based on twin or double occupancy

 

Important: The above programmes are sample itineraries can be tailor-made to suit the needs and wishes of our guests with advance warning.
